# NIN DEFENDER — Ideas

## Power-ups & Weapons
- **Power-up combos** — stacking triple shot + rapid fire + death ray simultaneously for insane firepower
- **Bouncing bullets** — power-up that makes your shots ricochet off screen edges
- **Wingman drone** — a small AI companion ship that follows you and adds extra firepower
- **Rage mode** — when you die and respawn, 3 seconds of invincible + death ray combo

## Enemies & Bosses
- **Screen-wide boss attacks** — bosses fill the screen with bullet-hell patterns, force the player to weave through gaps
- **Enemy chain explosions** — when one enemy dies near others, it triggers a chain reaction
- **Screen flip** — at phase 7+, enemies occasionally come from the LEFT (behind you)

## Environment & Hazards
- **Gravity wells** — random black holes that bend your bullets and pull enemies into spirals

## NIN Theme
- **NIN Easter eggs** — type "HURT" during gameplay for god mode, "CLOSER" for a hidden boss

## Game Modes & Meta
- **Speed run timer** — show time per phase, encourage speedrunning each phase
